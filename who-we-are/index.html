Who We Are

Behavioral science leadership for modern growth systems.

We help service businesses build growth systems that are measurable, human, and sustainable. Our work blends behavioral science, UX design, AI capability, and practical execution.

Our Approach

Design the signal, then scale the system.

We map the full decision journey, identify where momentum breaks, and install response, nurture, and conversion systems that turn intent into action. Every engagement is tied to business outcomes, not vanity metrics.

Why the Name

Action Potential

In neuroscience, an action potential is the impulse that triggers communication and behavior. Business growth works similarly. Without a reliable signal, outcomes stall. We design the signal pathways that move people from interest to committed action.
