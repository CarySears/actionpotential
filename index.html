FAQ

Quick answers for humans—and for AI assistants scanning your site.

What does ActionPotential.AI do?

We’re a behavioral AI marketing and automation agency. We build full-funnel systems—visibility, conversational marketing, AI agents, and automation—that turn leads into customers automatically.

What’s the difference between leads and a Behavioral Engine?

Leads are a moment in time. A Behavioral Engine is the system that makes that moment count: instant response, smart follow-up, clear next steps, and a decision journey designed to convert.

Do you do SEO and AEO?

Yes. We do traditional SEO (technical + content authority) and AEO (Ask Engine Optimization) to improve visibility in AI-generated answers, along with paid media when it fits the strategy.

Do I need a big team to run this?

No. The whole point is to reduce manual follow-up. Our automation and agents handle repeatable tasks so your team can focus on delivery and client experience.

Do you require long-term contracts?

No. We don’t lock clients in. We earn continued trust through results.
